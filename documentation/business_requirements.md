## Business Objective

Build an enterprise-grade Power BI analytics solution to provide executives and business leaders with visibility into sales performance, profitability, inventory efficiency, and customer retention across regions and channels.

## Key Business Questions
- Are we profitable after discounts, returns, and currency conversion?
- Which products and categories drive true margin?
- How efficiently is inventory being utilized?
- Are promotions increasing incremental sales?
- Are customers being retained over time?

## Success Criteria
- Single source of truth KPIs
- Consistent time intelligence
- Scalable data model
- Performance-optimized DAX
